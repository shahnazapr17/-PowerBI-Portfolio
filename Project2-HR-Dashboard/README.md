# 👥 HR Workforce Analytics Dashboard

## Overview
A people analytics dashboard built in Power BI analysing 
1,470 employees across departments, identifying attrition 
patterns and workforce trends.

## Key Metrics
- Total Employees: 1,470
- Attrition Count: 237
- Attrition Rate: 16.12%
- Average Salary: £6.5K/month
- Average Age: 37

## Key Insights
- 16.12% attrition rate — above industry average of 10-12%
- 25-34 age group has highest attrition
- Research & Development has highest attrition by department
- Nearly 40% of employees report low job satisfaction
- Laboratory Technicians have highest attrition by job role

## Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop
- DAX (CALCULATE, COUNTROWS, DIVIDE, AVERAGE)
- Power Query
- Calculated Columns (Age Group segmentation)

## Visuals Included
- KPI Cards
- Bar Chart (Attrition by Department)
- Bar Chart (Attrition by Job Role)
- Column Chart (Attrition by Age Group)
- Donut Chart (Job Satisfaction split)
- Donut Chart (Gender split)
- Line Chart (Attrition by Years at Company)
- Department Slicer
